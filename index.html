Welcome to the official Parent Teacher Organization page of Thomas L. Reeves Elementary School!

This page is a place for parents to connect. You will find school updates and reminders here, plus volunteer opportunities and other news from the PTO and school staff. We are located at 214 St. Augustine Drive in Long Beach, MS. We are a K-3rd grade school with approximately 400 students, and our principal is Jennifer Blackwell.


Our Values
We believe that:
  • Mutual trust, respect and integrity are critical for success.
  • All individuals are important and worth of dignity and respect.
  • Every person can achieve growth.
  • Expectations, motivation and engagement create a foundation for achievement.
  • Everyone will feel empowered to create a safe and positive environment that facilitates growth.
  • A quality education is best achieved through active and meaningful partnerships.
